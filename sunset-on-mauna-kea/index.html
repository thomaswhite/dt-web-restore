First Post… Yay!

This shot is from Mauna Kea on the Big Island of Hawaii. It is at 13,800' and it is quite cold up there. This is my first video and I have developed quite a few techniques since. This is very much worth watching, but you would also do well to watch more recent ones to see new tips and techniques.
