Creative Spot Removal

This edit is of the George Washington bridge looking toward Manhattan. The sky was cloudy and grey, but I made the best of the flat light to bring out the shadows. The bridge structure is a great place to show the power of lowpass filters and enhancing shading. I had some sensor dust and there were distracting birds and such to remove from some tricky areas. All in good fun and I like how it turned out.

If you'd like to follow along, here is the original RAW file
